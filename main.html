Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a classic film directed by Frank Darabont, based on the novella by Stephen King. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongly convicted of murder and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Through hope, friendship, and resilience, Andy navigates life behind bars, maintaining his dignity and eventually finding redemption.

Actors

The movie features outstanding performances by Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding. Their portrayal of friendship and perseverance in the face of adversity is truly captivating.
